Happy Avocado

Description

A good recipe for bored people with an avocado lying around.

Ingredients

  • one avocado
  • some jokes
  • 1kg of patience
  • determination as needed

Steps

  1. Take the avocado and say hi.
  2. It won't reply, because it's an avocado. That's okay!
  3. Try to appreciate the nice things about this avocado.
  4. Maybe roll it around?
  5. Accept that it's an avocado and it's just.. going to sit there.
  6. Tell it a couple of jokes. It can't judge you! It's an avocado!
  7. Repeat indefinitely.
