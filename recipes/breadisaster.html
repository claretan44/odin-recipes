Breadisaster

If you have some bread and want to make it question your culinary choices, this is the recipe for you!

Ingredients

  • bread of choice
  • jam/jelly
  • soy sauce
  • gochujang or ssamjang
  • green onions
  • a marshmallow

Steps

  1. Take the bread and compliment it. It's gonna need confidence to pull this recipe off.
  2. Drench the bread in the soy sauce.
  3. Place the gochujang, ssamjang, or condiment of your choice on top.
  4. Follow up with the jam/jelly.
  5. Top with the marshmallows.
  6. Sprinkle with green onions so it looks pretty.
