Soupy Fried Rice

Description

Need to dispose of a bunch of silken tofu? This recipe will give you fried rice with the consistency of porridge: it's weird, unique, and surprisingly not bad!

Ingredients

  • day old rice
  • minced garlic
  • 1 egg
  • salt, sugar
  • silken tofu
  • korean seasoned seaweed or garnish of choice
  • soy sauce
  • mentsuyu

Steps

  1. Beat the egg with a pinch of salt and around 1/3 of a spoonful of sugar.
  2. Heat up a frying pan, and add the garlic, egg, and rice with enough oil so it doesn't all burn.
  3. Mix in the silken tofu.
  4. Add roughly a spoonful of soy sauce and 1-2 spoons of mentsuyu to taste, enough so that it isn't a flavorless mess.
  5. Top with your chosen garnish!
